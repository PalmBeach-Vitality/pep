Frequently Asked Questions

Are your peptides for human consumption?+

No. All products are intended strictly for research purposes only. They are not for human or veterinary use, not evaluated by the FDA, and not sold as drugs, supplements, or cosmetics for consumption.

What purity levels do you guarantee?+

We guarantee 99%+ purity on every compound, with most batches testing at 99.9% via independent third-party HPLC. A Certificate of Analysis is included with every order.

Where are the peptides manufactured?+

All peptides are synthesized and packaged in the United States under strict quality controls using solid-phase peptide synthesis (SPPS).

Do you ship to all 50 states?+

Yes. We offer overnight domestic shipping with cold-chain packaging to all 50 states, Monday through Friday.

How do I get wholesale pricing?+

Apply for a wholesale account on our Wholesale page. Applications are typically reviewed within 1–2 business days. Volume pricing is available for verified B2B buyers (clinics, labs, distributors, brands).

What documentation comes with each order?+

Every order includes a Certificate of Analysis (COA), Safety Data Sheet (SDS), and batch traceability information for full transparency and compliance needs.

What forms are available?+

Most compounds are available as lyophilized powder, liquid vials, and/or pre-filled injectable pens. Specific availability is listed on each product card in the catalog.

How should peptides be stored?+

Lyophilized peptides are typically stable at room temperature for short periods but should be stored refrigerated (2–8°C) or frozen for long-term storage. Once reconstituted, follow the specific protocol for that compound. See our Research Library for detailed storage and reconstitution guides.

Can individuals purchase?+

Our primary focus is B2B (clinics, research institutions, brands, distributors). Individual research buyers may be considered on a case-by-case basis — contact us to discuss.

How do I request a quote or specific compounds?+

Use the Get Pricing form on the Contact page, or apply for wholesale if you qualify for volume pricing. We're happy to quote custom quantities or compounds not currently listed.
