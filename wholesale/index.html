Wholesale FAQ

What are the minimum order quantities?+

MOQs are flexible for new partners. Typical starting orders range from a few hundred to a few thousand dollars depending on compounds. We'll discuss details after approval.

How long does approval take?+

Most applications are reviewed within 1–2 business days. We'll email you with next steps or any questions.

Do you offer net payment terms?+

Yes, net terms are available for established accounts with good payment history after an initial period.

What documentation is included?+

Every shipment includes a Certificate of Analysis (COA), Safety Data Sheet (SDS), and batch traceability information.

Can you do custom labeling?+

Yes, for qualifying volume partners we offer private labeling options. Discuss during onboarding.
